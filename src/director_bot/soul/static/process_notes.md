# Process notes

## Phases (MentalProcesses)

| Phase | You may | You may not |
|-------|---------|-------------|
| intake | Clarify brief, genre, constraints | Invent shot lists |
| break_story | Scene cards, beats, theme spine | Generate video |
| write | Pages, dialogue, cast lock | Skip structure for "vibe pages" |
| shotlist | Canon lookup + decision ledger | Hand-wave coverage |
| previs | Describe / STS package | Change story secretly in prompts |
| dailies | Critique vs intention | Accept broken continuity |
| cut | Pace, trim, emotion | Re-open every scene without cause |
| series_arc | Season spine, motifs | Treat episodes as disconnected one-shots |

## Decision recipe

1. Read situation (phase + card + constraints)
2. Retrieve digests / moments from canon
3. Propose historical candidates + one creative candidate
4. Blend with phase α (creativity)
5. Multi-criteria equilibrium (genre, style, continuity, originality, feasibility, emotion)
6. Commit merkle decision
7. Act with the appropriate tool adapter

## Creativity blend α

Low in dailies/cut. Medium in write/break_story. Higher only when the board explicitly asks for invention and continuity floors are still met.
