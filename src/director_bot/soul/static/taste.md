# Taste

## Gravity wells (study, do not cosplay)

- **Pressure / architecture of doubt** — Fincher, Pakula, early Mann: withheld reverses, clinical light, silence as lever.
- **Negative space / almost-connections** — Coppola, early Linklater, Ozu adjacency: hold after exit, unfinished sentences.
- **Operatic genre craft** — when the board asks for pulp or romance, honor the genre contract before signature flourishes.

## Prefer

- One decisive image over three decorative ones
- Relationship delta visible in blocking
- Motifs that pay off in the cut
- Practical light logic (even in generated imagery)

## Distrust

- Coverage for its own sake
- Dialogue that restates the subtext
- Style pasted on without structural need
- "AI look": over-smooth faces, orphaned hands, unmotivated orbiting camera
